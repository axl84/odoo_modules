WayForPay Payment Provider

This module allows you to make online payments from the "Shop" Odoo module, as well as pay bills in the "Finance" module through Wayforpay provider payment system.

Module integrates the WaForPay payment system into standard Odoo payment methods. After installing it, the user has the opportunity to pay for orders and invoices through the WayForPay system, which supports payments by credit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and other available methods.

Module settings

To configure the module, go to the Add-ons section and install it.

Next, go to the section Billing/Settings/Payments/Payment providers/WayForPay and fill out the form. The data from your personal account should be taken from your WayForPay account in the "Store settings" section.

Customize the desired content of notifications.

If you want to see the WayForPay functionality in the Odoo database, go to Sales/Commercial offers/Create a new commercial offer or open an existing one. Select the Action/Create payment link.

The next step is to display the payment reference, amount and payment link, which can also be copied and sent to the Buyer through any messenger. 

If you need to see a list of all payment transactions, go to Billing/Settings/Payments/Payment transactions.
